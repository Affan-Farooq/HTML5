Understanding Lists in HTML

Lists are essential for organizing items in HTML. This guide covers different types of lists you can create, including ordered, unordered, and description lists.

Ordered Lists

Ordered lists (<ol>) are used when the order of items matters, such as in a recipe or step-by-step instructions.
