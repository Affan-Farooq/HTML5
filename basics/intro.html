1. Text Elements

HTML offers various text elements to structure and format content. Here are some commonly used ones:

Headings

HTML provides six levels of headings, from <h1> to <h6>, to organize content hierarchy.

Paragraphs

Use the <p> tag to create paragraphs of text. This helps in structuring long pieces of text in a readable way.

Bold and Italic

You can use <strong> or <b> for bold text and <em> or <i> for italic text.
